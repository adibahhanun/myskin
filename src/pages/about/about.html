About MySkin 1.0

by Adibah Hanun for Final Year Project
Version: 1.0

MySkin is your everyday reliable beauty application that enable you to analyze your skin type, look-up for halal and safe products and find out your everyday skin needs. Validated questionnaires and OCR technology are used to carefully analyze the user's skin type. Both the methods will generate a list of reliable, safe and halal products suggestion. A list of ingredients will also be listed along with the products. This list would be beneficial to Muslims as they can make sure that the ingredients are halal to be used. Besides that, products with unallowed chemical substances would not be listed.
